Simple Steps to Make a Difference

Reporting a stray or injured animal is quick and easy. Follow these steps to help an animal in need.

1
📋
Report
Submit a report of an abandoned or injured animal with photo and location details.
2
📍
Location
We track the animal's location in real-time so responders know exactly where to go.
3
🔔
Notify
Nearby volunteers and shelter partners are instantly notified to coordinate rescue.
4
🏠
Connect
Rescued animals are matched with loving adopters through our adoption network.
